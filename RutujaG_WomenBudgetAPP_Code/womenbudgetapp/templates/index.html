WomenBudgetApp

WE BELIEVE WOMEN ARE THE BEST MONEY MANAGERS!

Women save in small amounts for future. How would they know if saving that much amount will help meet their goals? WBA is a money manager for women, helping them to fine tune their finances by creating goals, setting budgets and contributing in small amount towards it. This application will also help in predicting the wealth which would get accumulated based on their goal term.
